How it works

  • 01. Get in touch
    Tell us about your venue and goals — or just say hi. We'll recommend the right approach over a quick, no-pressure call.
  • 02. We match your creators
    We handpick local food creators from our vetted network who match your restaurant's style, cuisine, and target audience. No random placements.
  • 03. Creators visit and create
    Creators dine at your venue and produce authentic content — Reels, TikToks, posts, and stories — that reaches their engaged local following naturally.
  • 04. New faces at the table
    Real visibility with real local audiences. New bookings, new followers, and new regulars who found you through a voice they already trust.
