Why Influencer Marketing?

Traditional advertising tells people about you. Influencer marketing shows them — through a voice they already follow and take dinner recommendations from. A local creator sharing their genuine experience isn't an ad. It's a recommendation.

  • Authentic by design
    Real people, real experiences — no scripted ads, no stock photography. Creator content converts because audiences trust it.
  • Locally targeted reach
    Brisbane-based creators with local, engaged audiences who are actively looking for their next favourite restaurant. Relevant reach, not just broad reach.
  • Content that keeps working
    Every Reel, TikTok, and post continues driving discovery long after the campaign ends. One creator visit can generate weeks of organic visibility.
  • Trackable results
    Clear reporting on what was posted, who saw it, and how it performed. No guesswork — just real numbers from real campaigns.
