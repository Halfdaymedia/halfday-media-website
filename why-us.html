Why Influencer Marketing?

Traditional advertising tells people about you. Influencer marketing shows them — through a voice they already follow, trust, and take dinner recommendations from.

When a local food creator walks into your restaurant, films their honest experience, and shares it with thousands of engaged Brisbane followers — that's not an ad. That's a recommendation. And recommendations convert.

  • Authentic by design
    Creator content feels natural because it is. No scripted ads, no stock photography — real people genuinely experiencing your venue, your food, and your team.
  • Locally targeted reach
    We work exclusively with Brisbane-based creators whose audiences are local, hungry, and already looking for their next favourite restaurant. Reach the right people, not just any people.
  • 60% higher engagement
    Micro-influencers consistently outperform large accounts on engagement rates. Smaller, tighter audiences mean stronger relationships — and better results for your restaurant.
  • Content that keeps working
    Every post, Reel, and TikTok created for your restaurant continues driving discovery long after the campaign ends. One visit can generate weeks of organic reach.
  • Trackable and transparent
    You'll know exactly what was posted, who saw it, and how it performed. No guesswork, no vanity metrics — real results you can see and measure.
