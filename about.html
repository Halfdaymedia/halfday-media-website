About Halfday Media

A Brisbane-based influencer marketing agency built entirely around restaurants — and the creators who love them.

What we do

Halfday Media connects Brisbane restaurants with local food creators who are genuinely passionate about the city's dining scene. We manage the whole process — identifying the right creators for your venue, coordinating visits, briefing content, and delivering a campaign that brings new faces through your door.

We're not a one-size-fits-all agency. Every campaign is tailored to your venue, your vibe, and the specific customers you want to attract.

Where we work

We're based in Brisbane and primarily serve restaurants across South East Queensland — but we work with brands Australia-wide for those ready to scale their creator marketing nationally.

Our creator network is built from scratch: local food lovers, lifestyle creators, and community voices with real, engaged followings in Brisbane. No fake numbers, no offshore content.

The person behind it

Halfday Media was founded by Josie — a Brisbane creative with a background in influencer marketing and a genuine love for the local food and hospitality scene.

After years working in the content and creator space, Josie noticed a consistent gap: Brisbane's extraordinary restaurant scene was being underserved by agencies that didn't truly understand hospitality. The briefs were off, the creators didn't fit the venue, and the results fell flat.

Halfday Media was built to fix that. A boutique agency with one focus — helping great restaurants find the audience they deserve, through creators who actually care about the experience.

How it works

  • 01. You choose a package
    Pick the campaign that suits your venue and goals — or reach out and we'll recommend the right fit together over a quick call.
  • 02. We match your creators
    We handpick local food creators from our vetted network who match your restaurant's style, cuisine, aesthetic, and target audience. No random placements.
  • 03. Creators visit and create
    Creators dine at your venue and produce authentic content — Reels, TikToks, posts, and stories — that reaches their engaged local following naturally and organically.
  • 04. New faces at the table
    Your restaurant gets real visibility with real local audiences. You see new bookings come in, new followers appear, and new regulars begin to build.
